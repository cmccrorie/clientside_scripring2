Why did we create Petal?

"We wanted a markup language for writing our technical documentation in. We looked around at a few options - DocBook, DITA, Markdown, and ReStuctured Text.
They were either too complicated, or too simple. We settled on LwDITA (Light-weight DITA) as a good middle ground,
i.e. not too complex, not too simple. However there is no good (free, open source, and easy to use) visual editor for
LwDITA, and we wanted people to be able to easily contribute to the technical documentation.
Therefore I decided that we should build an editor for LwDITA - and from there, Petal was born."
Adam Retter Director of Evolved Binary Ltd
